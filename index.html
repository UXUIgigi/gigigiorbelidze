About Me

Hello! I'm Gigi Giorbelidze, a passionate Product Designer and Game Artist with a love for pixel-perfect designs and user-centered solutions.

Product Design UX/UI Game Art Pixel Art Design Systems User Research

I specialize in creating intuitive digital experiences that bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality. With expertise in UX/UI design, design systems, and game art, I enjoy crafting engaging interfaces that tell stories and solve real user problems.

My approach combines thorough research, iterative design processes, and attention to the smallest details. When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new design tools, contributing to design communities, or creating pixel art that brings digital worlds to life.
